Welcome to Dzuwa Arts Company

This is the official Mobile App of Dzuwa Arts Company

Our Aim is to change the negative mindset that many Malawians have towards local productions that they can not emerge into the mainstream of international interest. We intend to take arts as an important weapon to fight social injustice and contribute to the social-economic development of the nation. We intend to turn talent into cash and take arts as one way of entrepreneurship.
